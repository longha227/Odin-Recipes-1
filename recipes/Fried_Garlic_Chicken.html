Garlic Chicken Fried Chicken

  • ⅔2 teaspoons garlic powder, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprikali>
  • ½ cup seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ 1 egg
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded thin
  • 1 cup oil for frying, or as needed

Step to make fried garlic chicken

  1. mix together the garlic powder, pepper, salt, paprika, bread crumbs and flour
  2. dip chicken into the egg and milk
  3. fried tyhe dipped chicken in an pre heat 350 degree F skillset within oil for 5 minutes
  4. dry the oil with the paper towel and enjoy the food
